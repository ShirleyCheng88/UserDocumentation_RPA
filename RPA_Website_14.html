EXE Connection Example

Connect to Power Supply using EXE

1. Open the drop-down list for the Executable path.

2. Select PathWave BenchVue Power Supply from the list and click open.

The drop-down list for the Executable path in The EXE Connection

EXE connection example to PathWave BenchVue Power Supply

3. Click Connect, and you will see PathWave BenchVue Power Supply is now connected to RPA successfully.
